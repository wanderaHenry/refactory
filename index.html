About Refactory

Refactory is a pioneer training academy in Uganda, offering alternative industry-led skilling for the global technology industry. It was established through a partnership between Laboremus Uganda Ltd, Fontes Foundation, Youth With A Mission UK, and Clarke International University, with support from the Norwegian Agency for International Development (NORAD).

These programs are designed to bridge the gap between education and industry, providing students with hands-on experience and practical skills that meet the demands of the tech industry.

Our Impact

Over the past five years, Refactory has trained approximately 700 young professionals. The academy is committed to inclusivity, featuring participants from underserved communities, including women, youth, and persons with disabilities.
